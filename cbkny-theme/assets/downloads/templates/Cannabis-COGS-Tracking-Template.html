Template Instructions & Best Practices

How to Use This Template Effectively

Setup Instructions
üîß Initial Setup
  1. Download the Excel template from the website
  2. Customize cost categories to match your business
  3. Set up allocation percentages for facility costs
  4. Choose inventory valuation method (FIFO, LIFO, or weighted average)
  5. Test formulas with sample data
Monthly Data Entry

Step 1: Enter all direct materials purchased during the month

Step 2: Record all direct labor hours and rates

Step 3: Allocate facility costs based on production percentage

Step 4: Update inventory valuations

Step 5: Review calculations for accuracy

Best Practices
  • Consistency is key - Use the same allocation methods throughout the year
  • Document everything - Keep receipts and supporting documentation
  • Review monthly - Don't wait until year-end to review calculations
  • Backup regularly - Keep multiple copies of your template
  • Consult professionals - Have a CPA review your calculations
Common Mistakes to Avoid
‚ùå Common Mistakes
  • Including non-deductible expenses in COGS
  • Inconsistent allocation methods
  • Missing direct labor costs
  • Incorrect facility cost allocations
  • Not documenting allocation methodologies
